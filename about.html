About Me

Hello! I’m an Electrical/Computer Engineering Technology student who enjoys programming, designing components, and thoughtful user experiences. I focus on clarity and maintainability. The way I got into all this stuff is because my father and siblings have used devices and made things that also got me interested.

Outside of coding, I enjoy learning, designing physical projects, and tinkering with tools. My grandfather is one of a dozen music box restorers left which made me want to learn about the hands-on making process.
